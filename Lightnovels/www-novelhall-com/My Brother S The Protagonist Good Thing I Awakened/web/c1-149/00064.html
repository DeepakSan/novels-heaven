For a moment, he felt like a rabbit looking at a tiger.

"You talk big for a dead guy!"

Without waiting for another word, he lunged at Feng Chen, ready to punch Feng Chen with his full strength.

His speed was impressive-too fast for those in the Core Formation Stage and below.

Many in the crowd gasped, expecting Feng Chen to be struck down instantly.

But Feng Chen didn't move. His eyes remained calm, his body still.

And just when Bai Zhang's attack was inches from landing, Feng Chen's hand shot out, faster than a lightning strike, grabbing Bai Zhang's wrist in midair.

The air seemed to freeze.

Bai Zhang's momentum came to a screeching halt, his body lurching forward as he tried to force his strength down on Feng Chen.

But it was no use.

Bai Zhang's eyes widened in shock as he felt his power being effortlessly neutralized.

He tried to pull away, but Feng Chen's grip was like iron, unmoving and unyielding.

The crowd fell silent, many unable to believe what they were seeing.

They didn't think that Feng Chen would be able to stop Bai Zhang's punch.

One should know that the Three Elements Divine Realm punch was nothing to scoff at and could even break steel.
