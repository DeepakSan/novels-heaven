Chapter 123  Feng Chen Takes The Win! People in the Tianlong Mansion also watched as Feng Chen reached the finish line with an overwhelming display of speed. Their eyes locked on Feng Chen, who had a calm expression on his face.

His expression remained unchanged, undisturbed by Tianlong Qi's attack, his victory in this round, or even his triumph over the entire competition.

It was like nothing would faze him in this world.

"Haha… The Family Master did it! We have won!"

Feng Zhenshan exclaimed excitedly.

He was worried the entire time from when Feng Chen was surrounded by other clan leaders to when Tianlong Qi attacked him.

Even then, he kept a calm expression, refusing to disgrace his clan with an unseemly reaction. But now, his pent-up emotions burst forth, and he couldn't hold back a shout.

As soon as Feng Zhenshan said those words, Tianlong Elder's expression instantly turned gloomy with some even glaring at the Feng Clan Member.

"Clan Leader has won! We have secured first place."

Feng Jianhong also couldn't help but shout out in joy.

Feng Mei, Feng Liang, Feng Xiaoyu, and even Feng Yun joined in the excitement. Their win wasn't a cheap victory as they had taken first place in all the rounds without any exception.

They had clearly dominated the Eastern Gathering and showed everyone just how powerful their Feng Clan was.

The other people also looked at Feng Chen with a surprised look. They finally realized that they had underestimated this young leader of the Feng Clan.

Not only did he calmly deal with Bai Zhang and other clan leaders but he also casually took on an attack from Peak Three Elements Divine Realm cultivator.

Who could have thought that Feng Chen, who is just 30 years old, was hiding so deeply?

They thought it was no wonder that two Zhou Elders had fallen into the hands of Feng Chen.

Ice Fairy also has a look of surprise on her face. She has been observing Feng Chen throughout this round and could say that Feng Chen is a genius.

Even she, a Nascent Soul Realm cultivator couldn't see through him and even struggled to see just how Feng Chen was able to come out unscathed from Tianlong Qi's attack.

Of course, one of the reasons she couldn't tell was because she was watching it in Heavenly Projection Crystal.

Otherwise, if she had been present there, she definitely would have known with her senses.

( His power has exceeded that of the Three Elements Divine Realm. But he didn't seem to have broken through to Nascent Soul Realm. )

Although she didn't think Feng Chen was Nascent Soul Realm, she concluded that he was infinitely close to reaching it.

This realization shocked her even more. 30 year old Nascent Soul Realm cultivator? He might just be the youngest to reach Nascent Soul Realm in the Azure Sky Empire.

She knew that she had to reassess the importance that her sect gave to the Feng Clan. They could turn out to be much more powerful than what they previously thought.

On the battlefield, Feng Chen stood there without doing anything for a few seconds before his eyes flickered over to where Tianlong Qi and Yuan Hao were.

Tianlong Qi and Yuan Hao finally reached the finish line, panting and exhausted.

The second one to reach was Yuan Hao and Tianlong Qi ended up in third place.

The reason for that was because Tianlong Qi wasted his Qi to attack Feng Chen and he was also late to react after Feng Chen had taken the first place.

Yuan Hao took advantage of those things and bagged second place.

Tianlong Qi shot Feng Chen a glare, but Feng Chen simply raised an eyebrow, unbothered. Tianlong Qi's qi crackled in his hands as he stood there, staring at Feng Chen's back, unable to comprehend how he dodged his attack. The Earth Grade Artifact had been his trump card, the one advantage he had hoped would ensure his victory. Yet, in the blink of an eye, it had been rendered meaningless.

Tianlong Qi clenched his fists tightly, the rage bubbling within him. He couldn't accept it!

How could he accept that someone like Feng Chen had defeated him despite all the preparations he made?

"YOU CHEATED!"

Tianlong Qi yelled as he quickly approached Feng Chen.

"Admit it! You used some underhanded method!"

Feng Chen merely sighed and didn't even think about replying to him. You always meet such people who themselves use an underhanded method but when they fail, they start accusing the other party.
