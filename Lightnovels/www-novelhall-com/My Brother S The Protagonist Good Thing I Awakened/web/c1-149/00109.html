Chapter 110  Rank 2 Formation Master! Tianlong Qi still could not accept reality and felt like he was dreaming. Otherwise, he believes that his well-thought-out plan wouldn't fail and that he wouldn't have lost One Million Gold Coins just like that.

Feng Chen, observing Tianlong Qi's distress, allowed a subtle smile to grace his lips. He knew full well that Feng Yun's power wasn't something that could be easily matched by common sense.

After all, he was the protagonist!

( Do you think my brother is called the protagonist for no reason? Losing simply isn't part of his vocabulary. )

Moreover, he knew that with Ethereal Frost Flame, the formation stood no chance. He was impressed that Feng Yun was able to control Ethereal Frost Flame despite using much of its power.

If he could fully control the Ethereal Frost Flame, its power would be far greater, potent enough to potentially break even a Rank-4 Formation.

But it wasn't an easy feat.

In any case, he couldn't pass up the chance to mock Tianlong Qi, who had been brimming with confidence and arrogance just moments before.

"Well, it appears the thrill you were looking for has arrived, Tianlong Clan Leader." Feng Chen said.

What could be more devastating than beginning to gloat, only to realize you've lost before you could even finish boasting?

Tianlong Qi, his composure slipping, forced himself to scowl, turning to Feng Chen with a sharp, defensive glare.

With each passing second, Tianlong Qi's earlier excitement began to sour. The realization that his well-laid plans were unraveling before his eyes gnawed at him. He had been so focused on the possibility of victory that he hadn't considered the consequences of his failure. As he watched the projection, he couldn't deny the truth; Feng Yun had broken through the Formation. Tianlong Qi clenched his fists tighter, the sharp pain in his palms a welcome distraction from the turmoil inside. "This isn't over!" He vowed silently. He needed to regain control of the situation and salvage his dignity before it slipped away even further.

"Hehe… Tianlong Clan Leader, don't forget about our bet, and remember to give me my One Million Gold Coins, otherwise, for each hour you delay, there is a 10% interest!"

Feng Chen spoke loudly enough for everyone to hear. Now that Tianlong Qi had lost, he wasn't about to let him off easily and began demanding the money almost immediately.

As for whether he could manage to gather such an amount in little time, it wasn't any of his concern.

Tianlong Qi's face contorted with barely contained fury at Feng Chen's mocking words. The crowd's excitement didn't help either; they watched intently, sensing that the tension between the two Clan Leaders was far from over.

Some members of the audience snickered, amused by the predicament Tianlong Qi found himself in, while others couldn't help but marvel at Feng Chen's blatant disrespect of the Tianlong Clan.

"Ten percent interest?"

"The Feng Clan Leader isn't just strong—he's ruthless with his terms too."

"Haha… since he dares to bet, he should be able to take the loss."

"I'll pay," Tianlong Qi snapped, his voice cracking just slightly. There was nothing he could do at the moment. With so many clans witnessing the event and the Icy Veil Sect present, there was no way he could backtrack on his promise.
