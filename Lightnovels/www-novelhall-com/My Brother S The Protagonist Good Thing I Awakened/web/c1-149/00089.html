Chapter 90  Skyfire Feather The auction continued, with a few smaller items coming and going, none capturing Feng Yun's attention.

They were all the items that were difficult to obtain for normal people but not that hard for someone of his stature.

"Esteemed guests, the next item is a true rarity and one of our most prized possessions of the evening." The auctioneer paused, letting the suspense build. With a dramatic flourish, an attendant brought out a small, carefully sealed box. When it opened, the crowd gasped. Inside, resting upon a bed of black silk, was a radiant feather. Its fiery red and gold hues shimmered under the light, giving off a faint but palpable warmth that seemed to pulse with a life of its own.

"A Skyfire Feather!"

The crowd erupted into murmurs and gasps of astonishment. "Skyfire Feather is said to contain a fragment of the ancient phoenix's life force," The auctioneer continued, "and can be used in high-level alchemy or to heal injuries. For some, it may even hold the key to awakening a hidden bloodline."

The hall fell into a stunned silence. Even the most powerful figures in the room sat up straighter, their interest piqued.

Indeed, after all that boring stuff, Azure Sky Chamber Of Commerce was finally bringing out the big gun.

"This… "

Feng Mei's heart skipped a beat the moment the feather was revealed. Without knowing why, she felt a deep connection to it, as if the relic was calling out to her, resonating with something deep within. Her hands clenched slightly on the armrest of her seat, her eyes fixed on the Phoenix Feather.

Feng Yun, sitting beside her, noticed her sudden change in demeanor. "Mei'er, what's wrong?"

"I… I don't know!" Feng Mei whispered, her voice unsteady. "That feather… it feels like… it has some connection with me."

Feng Yun's eyes narrowed. "You feel drawn to it?"

Feng Mei nodded, her gaze never leaving the feather. "I can't explain it, but it feels like it belongs to me."

Feng Yun glanced at his brother Feng Chen, who had also noticed Feng Mei's reaction. Although his brother has said that he would buy each of them something that they want, Skyfire Feather was highly sought after and it would cost a fortune to obtain one.

On the other hand, Feng Chen wasn't particularly concerned about losing the bid. He understood why Feng Mei felt such a strong connection to the Skyfire Feather; it was often said to be a feather from a phoenix.

Although some Skyfire Feathers were believed to be true phoenix feathers, others came from creatures with a mixed phoenix bloodline, which is of inferior quality.

Although of inferior quality, the effects of those Skyfire Feather were similar. They could heal injuries and could even awaken the physique of some people.

If Feng Mei had a reaction to it, then Feng Chen thought that this Skyfire Feather must be of high quality and must be connected to the real phoenix.

( I must get this! )

Feng Chen thought. Even if Feng Mei didn't want it, he had made up his mind to get it. A Real Phoenix Feather!

Its value couldn't be measured in mere Gold Coins and even in the System Store, it cost more than a hundred thousand System Points.

However, he knew the chances were slim, as her Jade Phoenix physique was a top-tier bloodline, even among phoenixes. Awakening such a powerful lineage with just the feather would require an extraordinary stroke of luck.

Their previous annual Clan income was only in the tens of thousands and now Feng Chen directly bid 200,000 gold coins for a single item.

It was like throwing away decades worth of income!

Feng Yun turned to Feng Mei with a smile. "It's yours now."

Feng Mei nodded slowly, her heart racing with a mixture of excitement and relief. She didn't know why, but having that Skyfire Feather felt… right, like a missing piece of herself had just been found.

However, she was also scared of what might happen if nothing happened. Then she would have made Feng Chen waste 200,000 gold coins.

Such a huge amount was something that she couldn't even begin to imagine.
