Feng Chen was very happy to receive the mission as he had previously used almost all of his System Points.

Moreover, he could get a large amount of System Points as long as his clan took first place in each round.

With Feng Yun, he wasn't very worried about the first three rounds. Though he couldn't guarantee that he could get first place every time as what the competition will be about is unknown to him.
