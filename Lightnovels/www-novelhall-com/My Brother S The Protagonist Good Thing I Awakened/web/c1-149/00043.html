Nestled high in the Cloudreach Mountains, the Mystic Flame Sect is renowned as the most prestigious and formidable sect in the Azure Sky Empire.

The sect's headquarters is located in a place where the mountains pierce the clouds, giving it an ethereal appearance.

Surrounded by vast stretches of ancient forests and powerful spiritual veins, the Mystic Flame Sect is not only a hub of cultivation but also a place of breathtaking beauty and awe-inspiring grandeur.

The sect's main complex is a masterpiece of architecture, built with gleaming white stone and adorned with intricate carvings of phoenixes and flames-symbolizing the sect's affinity with fire.

Deep within the sect's inner sanctum lies the Flame Hall, the most sacred place in Mystic Flame Sect.

It is here that the sect leader and elders gather to discuss and strategize.

The hall is said to be imbued with the ancient flames of the first sect leader, flames that have never been extinguished and are rumored to grant enlightenment and immense power to those deemed worthy.
