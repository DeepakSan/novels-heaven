Chapter 109  First Place Of Second Round Of Eastern Gathering Just as Tianlong Qi was savoring the moment, letting his thoughts drift to the rewards he was sure awaited him, a murmur began to ripple through the crowd. Eyes darted to the Heavenly Projection Crystal as the image shifted, refocusing on the face of the contestant who had broken through the formation.

A moment of stunned silence fell over the hall.

It wasn't Tianlong Ping or Tianlong Yu.

It was Feng Yun.

Eyes widened in shock as the image on the Heavenly Projection Crystal focused clearly, revealing Feng Yun standing triumphantly amidst the broken remnants of the Rank-2 Formation.

What caught everyone's attention even more was the roaring inferno that surrounded him, flames so intense that they painted the air with waves of heat.

The flames danced wildly, casting brilliant shades of crimson and gold, yet Feng Yun's expression was one of utter calm. He stood calm, his figure encased in a torrent of blazing energy, trying to control the flames. His clothes, though whipped around by the fierce heat, remained unburned, almost like the fire that was engulfing him wasn't a real fire.

"What… What kind of flame is that?" Someone in the crowd whispered, astonishment clear in their voice.

"It looks… It looks almost like it has a life of its own!" another murmured.

"Could it be… Spirit Flame?"

The crowd was sure that the Flame surrounding Feng Yun was an extraordinary flame and many thought that it might be Spirit Flames. No one thought it was Mystical Flame because having Spirit Flame itself was very rare, especially for Core Formation Stage cultivators.

And who would even think that someone like Feng Yun would possess Mythical Flame which even Nascent Soul Realm Cultivators are said to have problems controlling?

At least, not them!

The flames pulsed once as if trying to break free of Feng Yun's control, but he merely narrowed his eyes, exerting his will over the fire. The inferno obeyed, quieting its tumultuous nature.

PHEW!

Feng Yun let out a breath of relief, the flames simmering down around him and he could control the Ethereal Frost Flame again.

"Ethereal Frost Flame," He muttered to himself, his voice carrying a mix of awe and exhaustion. "It's really difficult to control."

He could control it well enough for pill refinement, but when it came to using it more intensely or tapping into its full power, he still struggled.

The reason why Feng Yun was able to quickly break through the Rank-2 Formation was because of the Ethereal Frost Flame.

He at first tried using different techniques but none worked. All of his attacks were absorbed by the formation and hit back.

He was almost seriously injured when he used the Heavenly Grade Technique and it was only thanks to his quick reaction that he barely dodged it.

Even though he managed to put a crack in the formation, it quickly repaired itself. He estimated that it would take around ten similar attacks to completely break through it using that method, which would obviously consume a lot of time.

Then he had an idea: he could try using flames to break through the formation. However, ordinary flames proved useless, as they were simply absorbed by the formation's protective barrier.

So, he proceeded to use Ethereal Frost Flame which was working, but it still wasn't powerful enough to completely shatter the Rank-2 Formation.

With no choice, he has to increase the intensity of the flame but that also means that he will need more control over the flame.

At this stage, he didn't yet have full control over the Ethereal Frost Flame at the intensity required to break the formation.
