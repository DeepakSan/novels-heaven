Feng Chen soon arrived at the auction office where he sold his items. He caught sight of the auctioneer and approached him.

The auctioneer, still dressed impeccably in his formal robes, greeted Feng Chen with a respectful bow.

"Clan Leader Feng, congratulations on securing such fine items today. How may I assist you further?" The auctioneer asked with a smile, though his eyes couldn't hide a hint of admiration for Feng Chen's spending power.

Feng Chen didn't waste time with pleasantries. "I've come to settle the accounts and retrieve the remaining funds!" The auctioneer nodded quickly. "Of course, of course. The sum you are owed is ready for collection." He gestured toward a set of storage rings laid out on a velvet tray. "I've had our staff prepare the remaining 1.5 million gold coins as instructed. Since this is your first time at our auction, we decided to take no commission and those storage rings could be considered a gesture of goodwill from us."

Feng Chen nodded slightly, appreciating the gesture. "If so, then thank you. I will remember your generosity."

The auctioneer bowed once more. "It's a pleasure doing business with the Feng Clan. Should you need anything else, do not hesitate to reach out to us."

"Sure!"

Feng Chen turned and left the room, his footsteps echoing down the hall. Feng Chen had just left when the manager rushed into the office moments later.

"Is the Feng Clan Leader gone?"

The manager asked hastily.

"Yes!"

The auctioneer remarked, taken aback by the manager, who appeared to have rushed in.

Although just the manager of Qingyun City's Azure Sky Chamber Of Commerce branch, he still held quite a bit of status and could be comparable to that of elders from Sect.

Furthermore, he was in the Grand Qi Condensation Realm, making him quite powerful, so it was surprising to see him in such a state.

"Looks like I was a few seconds late." The manager was visibly regretful.

The auctioneer, noticing the manager's distress, furrowed his brow and inquired. "Why the rush? Is there something important regarding the Feng Clan?"

The manager straightened up, his expression shifting from regret to seriousness. "You could say that. The Feng Clan's recent activities have been the topic of discussion among the more influential factions in the capital. They're gaining a reputation that we need to pay attention to."

"Even the head office said to me to pay attention to them during the Eastern Gathering."

"So, it wasn't just because of the Feng Clan selling those items?" The auctioneer echoed, intrigued. "Of course, that is also one of the factors. Although the Feng Clan Leader didn't tell me the real reason, it is for sure that they must have hunted those monsters."

"But… those are Sixth Grade Monsters! Do they even have the capability?"

The auctioneer asked in surprise. After all, Nascent Soul Realm cultivators are required for that and he didn't think that the Feng Clan would have one.

"Maybe! Though I am more inclined to think that they have the help from outsiders."

The manager replied. He also didn't think that the Feng Clan had Nascent Soul Realm Cultivators.

With a clan having only one Three Elements Divine Cultivator, how could he expect them to have a Nascent Soul realm Cultivator?
