Chapter 66: A Costly CompensationThe crowd sucked in a mouthful of cool air, this move by Feng Chen was considered a blatant provocation for Tianlong Qi!

"Has Feng Chen truly lost his mind?"

"He's truly challenging the Tianlong Master outright. This isn't a battle he can win."
